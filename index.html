About LadyBird Accounting Solutions

Your Trusted Partner in Financial Success

Founded by Samantha Taylor, LadyBird Accounting Solutions has been providing dedicated accounting services to small businesses since 2006. As a registered BAS Agent and certified professional, we bring nearly two decades of expertise to help your business thrive.

Our Mission

To empower and support small businesses by providing comprehensive and reliable accounting and payroll services. With a commitment to excellence, integrity, and personalised attention, we strive to streamline financial processes, ensuring accurate and compliant reporting. Our goal is to be a trusted partner, helping businesses focus on growth and success while we handle their financial needs with utmost professionalism and dedication.

Experience & Qualifications

  • Registered BAS Agent since 2007
  • Certified QuickBooks Online Pro Advisor
  • Xero Certified Partner
  • MYOB Partner
  • Accounting Certificate IV (TAFE NSW, 2006-2007)
  • Accounting Certificate II (TAFE NSW, 1997)
  • 19+ years of specialized small business accounting experience

Professional Background

With a career spanning over 30 years in finance and accounting, Samantha began her professional journey in 1994 with Sydney Ferries, gaining extensive experience in accounts payable, accounts receivable, and revenue management. After international experience at Reuters in Zurich, Switzerland, she returned to Australia and established LadyBird Accounting Solutions to focus exclusively on empowering small businesses.

This extensive background, combined with ongoing professional development and registration with the Tax Practitioners Board, ensures that your business receives the highest quality accounting services available.
